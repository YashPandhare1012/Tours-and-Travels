Amazing Andaman 5 Nights / 6 Days Tour

Day 1:

  • Arrival in Singapore & Night Safari

  • Arrive at Singapore Changi Airport and transfer to your hotel.
  • Check-in and relax after the journey.
  • In the evening, head to the famous Night Safari. Enjoy a tram ride through the park and spot a variety of nocturnal animals in their natural habitat.
  • After the safari, return to your hotel.
  • Over night stay in Singapore.

Day 2:

  • Singapore City Tour & Sentosa Island Adventure

  • After breakfast, enjoy a half-day Singapore city tour: Visit Merlion Park, Chinatown, Little India, and Marina Bay Sands.
  • In the afternoon, transfer to Sentosa Island and explore attractions like S.E.A. Aquarium, Madame Tussauds, and the Skyline Luge.
  • Return to the hotel in the evening.
  • Over night stay in Singapore.

Day 3:

  • Gardens by the Bay & Universal Studios Singapore

  • Morning visit to Gardens by the Bay to explore the Cloud Forest, Flower Dome, and Supertree Grove.
  • Head to Universal Studios Singapore for a thrilling day with various themed rides and shows.
  • Evening free to explore Clarke Quay or enjoy a river cruise.
  • Over night stay in Singapore.

Day 4:

  • Singapore to Kuala Lumpur(Travel)

  • After breakfast, check out from your hotel and transfer to Singapore Airport or the bus terminal for your journey to Kuala Lumpur (approx. 1-hour flight or 6-hour bus ride).
  • Arrive in Kuala Lumpur and transfer to your hotel.
  • Evening visit to the Petronas Twin Towers and KLCC Park.
  • Over night stay in Kuala Lumpur.

Day 5:

  • Kuala Lumpur City Tour & Batu Caves

  • After breakfast, explore Kuala Lumpur’s highlights: Visit the King’s Palace, National Monument, Independence Square, and the iconic KL Tower.
  • Visit Batu Caves, a famous Hindu temple and one of the city’s most popular landmarks.
  • Evening free to explore Jalan Alor Food Street for delicious local cuisine.
  • Over night stay in Kuala Lumpur

Day 6:

  • Genting High lands Excursion

  • Full-day trip to Genting Highlands, a hill resort just outside Kuala Lumpur.
  • Enjoy a scenic cable car ride, visit the Genting Premium Outlet, and explore Skytropolis Indoor Theme Park.
  • Return to Kuala Lumpur in the evening.
  • Over night stay in Kuala Lumpur.

Day 6:

  • Departure from Kuala Lumpur

  • Checkout from the hotel after breakfast.
  • Depending on your flight schedule, you can visit local markets like Central Market or Chinatown for some last-minute shopping.
  • Transfer to Kuala LumpurInternational Airport for your departure.
