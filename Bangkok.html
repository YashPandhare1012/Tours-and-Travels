Bangkok and Pattaya 4 Nights / 5 Days Tour

Day 1:

  • Arrive in Bangkok and Transfer to Pattaya.
  • Arrive at Bangkok Airport and transfer to Pattaya (approx. 2-hour drive).
  • Check into your Pattaya hotel and relax after the journey
  • In the evening, enjoy the Alcazar Cabaret Show, a dazzling performance showcasing vibrant costumes and cultural acts.

Day 2:

  • Coral Island Tour
  • After breakfast, head out for a half-day trip to Coral Island (Koh Larn).
  • Enjoy water activities like parasailing, jet skiing, or just relax on the beach.
  • Return to Pattaya by afternoon.
  • Evening at leisure to explore Pattaya’s nightlife or visit Walking Street.

Day 3:

  • Travel to Bangkok and Bangkok Sight seeing
  • Checkout from your Pattaya hotel and travel to Bangkok.
  • Check into your Bangkok hotel and take a short rest.
  • Visit the Golden Buddha (Wat Traimit) and Wat Pho, home to the famous Reclining Buddha statue.
  • Get on the.

Day 4:

  • Princess ChaoPhrayaDinnerCruise
  • Breakfast at the Hote
  • Day at Leisure: Spend the day exploring Bangkok’s markets, shopping centers, or sightseeing spots. You can visit malls like MBK Center or Siam Paragon.
  • Evening Dinner Cruise: In the evening, experience the Princess Chao Phraya Dinner Cruise. Dine on a delicious buffet while enjoying live entertainment and views of Bangkok’s illuminated river side temples and landmarks.
  • Over night in Bangkok

Day 5:

  • Departure
  • Depending on your flight time, enjoy a leisurely breakfast at the hotel.
  • You can opt for some last-minute shopping at local markets or malls like MBK Center or Siam Paragon.
  • Checkout from the hotel and transfer to Bangkok Airport for your departure.
