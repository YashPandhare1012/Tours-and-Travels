Dubai Dairies
5 Nights / 6 Days Tour

Day 1:

  • Arrival And Dhow cruise with dinner
  • Onarrival at Dubai International Airport / Sharjah International Airport, you'll be transferred to your hotel. Upon arrival, check in to the hotel and relax. In the evening get ready to experience the DhowCruise. Cruising down Dubai creek is a novel experience, one which you cannot miss when in Dubai. With the fascinating lights of Dubai Creek and the soft music in the background, sip your favorite drink and enjoy a buffet dinner onboard the cruise.

Day 2:

  • Half day Dubai city tour + Burj Khalifa 124 floor non-prime hour
  • After breakfast, get ready for a day full of sightseeing and experiences. First, proceed for a City tour. Experience the historic sites and vibrant cosmopolitan life of Dubai on this comprehensive sightseeing tour of the city. After this, you will be dropped at Dubai Mall for a visit to the Burj Khalifa. After that return to the hotel for a comfortable overnight stay.

Day 3:

  • Desert Safari with dinner
  • After breakfast, visit the famous Gold Souk .Later in the day, you will be picked up from the hotel for the Desert Safari around 03:00 PM. Experience the magic of desert safari in Dubai as we take you into the heart of the desert on an adventurous Roller coaster drive on the high dunes. Enjoy the thrill of dune bashing and camel ride. Also enjoy Belly Dancing and BBQ dinner. Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 4:

  • Miracle Garden + Global Village
  • After breakfast, visit Miracle Garden which occupies over 72,000 square meters, making it the world's largest natural flower garden, featuring over 50 million flowers and 250 million plants. In the evening, visit Global Village. Return to the hotel and overnight stay.

Day 5:

  • Abu Dhabi Sightseeing/City Tour + Ferrari World (Standard Ticket)
  • After breakfast, start your full-day Abu Dhabi sightseeing tour where you will visit, Qasr Al Watan. Marvel at the beauty of Arabian craftsmanship at the presidential palace of the capital, Qasr Al Watan. Proceed further by a visit to discover the emirate’s past with a visit to Heritage Village, a reconstruction of a traditional oasis village that features the desert way of life. End your tour by marveling at the grandiose Emirates Palace along the way and remember to take photos. After that visit, the most famous Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque- one of the world’s largest mosques, a palatial architectural wonder that is rising majestically from manicured gardens and visible from the bridges joining Abu Dhabi Island to the mainland, the Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque is an impressive welcome to the city. With more than 80 marble domes on a roof-line held aloft by 1000 pillars and punctuated by four 107m-high minarets, it's a masterpiece of modern Islamic architecture and design. Visit Ferrari World and enjoy the adventurous rides and thrill of sports racing. After seeing the most famous monument in Abu Dhabi, return to your hotel.

Day 6:May 20, 2024

  • Departure
  • After breakfast, check out from the hotel and get transferred to Sharjah Airport for your return flight. And now it's time to say goodbye with sweet memories and stay in touch for more such tours.
