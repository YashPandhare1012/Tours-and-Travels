Mysore Ooty 7Nights /8Days Tour

Day 1:

  • Arrive Bangalore or Coimbatore to Kodaikanal
  • Pickup from arrival and proceed to Kodaikanal. On arrival, check-in to the hotel. Evening visit to Cookers Walk, Green Valley View, Kodai Lake (star-shaped lake), and Bryant Park. Overnight stay at Kodaikanal.
  • Distance:Bangalore to Kodaikanal- 480 Kms /9Hoursbyroad
  • Distance:Coimbatore to Kodaikanal- 190 Kms /5.5 Hours byroad

Day 2:

  • Kodaikanal Local Sightseeing
  • After breakfast, visit Pillar Rocks, Silent Valley View, Berijam Lake View, Silver Cascade Falls, Devil’s Kitchen (Guna Caves), Pine Forest, and Kurinji Andavar Temple. Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.
  • (Note: Prior permission is needed for Berijam Lake View; coverage depends on availability).

Day 3:

  • Kodaikanal to Ooty Local Sightseeing
  • After breakfast, check out and proceed to Ooty. On arrival, check in to the hotel. Visit Rose Garden, Ooty Lake, and Botanical Garden. Overnight stay at Ooty
  • Distance:Kodaikanal to Ooty- 270 Kms/7Hours by road

Day 4:

  • Ooty &Coonoor Local Sightseeing
  • After breakfast, proceed to Coonoor for sightseeing: Sim’s Park, Lam’s Rock, Dolphin Nose, Ketti Valley (scenic views), Tea Factory, Tea Museum, and Doddabetta Peak (highest peak of Nilgiri mountains). Return to Ooty for an overnight stay
  • Distance: Ooty to Coonoor-50Kms/2 Hours by road

Day 5:

  • Ooty to Coorg
  • After breakfast, check out and proceed to Coorg. En route, visit NisargaDhama and the Tibetan Golden Temple. On arrival, check in to the hotel. In the evening, visit Raja’s Seat for scenic views. Overnight stay at Coorg.
  • Distance:Ooty to Coorg-265Kms/6.5 Hours by road

Day 6:

  • Coorg Local Sightseeing
  • After breakfast, visit Tala Kaveri Temple, Bhagamandala (Bhagandeshwara Temple), Omkareshwara Temple, Madikeri Fort, and Abbey Falls and Dubare Elephant Camp. Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 7:

  • Coorg to Mysore Local Sightseeing
  • After breakfast, check out and proceed to Mysore. En route, visit Saint Philomena's Church, Big Bull, Mysore Zoo, Chamundi Hill (Goddess Chamundeshwari), and Maharaja’s Mysore Palace. In the evening, visit Brindavan Gardens (botanical park with musical fountains). Overnight stay at Mysore.
  • Distance:Coorg to Mysore-130Kms/3.5 Hours by road

Day 8:

  • Mysore to Bangalore Drop
  • After breakfast, check out and proceed to Bangalore. En route, visit Srirangapatna (Dariya Daulat Bagh, Gumbaz, Tippu Fort, and Sri Ranganathaswamy Temple). Drop at Bangalore for your onward journey.
  • Distance:Mysore to Bangalore- 160 Kms/3.5 Hours by road
