PHUKET & Krabi 4Nights/5Days Tour

Phuket Krabi Package -

  • 02 Nights Deevana Plaza Krabi or similar with B/F – Krabi
  • 02 Nights Sunset Beach Resort Phuket or similar with B/F – Phuket

Day 1:

  • Arrival in Phuket and Transfer to Krabi
  • Arrive at Phuket Airport and meet your driver for a private transfer to your hotel in Krabi.on route have breakfast
  • Check-in at your Krabi hotel and spend the rest of the day at leisure have lunch
  • Explore the local area or relax on Ao Nang Beach. ● Overnight stay in Krabi.

Day 2:

  • 4 Island Tour in Krabi
  • After breakfast, head out for a full-day 4 Island Tour by longtail boat
  • Visit popular spots like Phra Nang Cave, Chicken Island, Poda Island, and Tup Island. Enjoy snorkeling, swimming, and a delicious lunch.
  • Return to your hotel by evening. ● Overnight stay in Krabi.

Day 3:

  • Krabi to Phuket & Leisure
  • After breakfast Check out from your Krabi hotel in the morning.
  • Private transfer to your Phuket hotel.
  • Check-in and relax.
  • Spend the evening exploring fantasia show , carnival magic show , Patong Beach or shopping at local markets.
  • Overnight stay in Phuket.

Day 4:

  • Phi Phi Island Tour
  • Enjoy breakfast at the hotel before departing for the Phi Phi Island tour by big boat (National Park Fees excluded).
  • Explore the beautiful islands, crystal-clear waters, and pristine beaches.
  • Enjoy lunch on board and free time for snorkeling and swimming.
  • Return to Phuket by evening. ● Overnight stay in Phuket.

Day 5:

  • Departure from Phuket
  • After breakfast, check out from the hotel.
  • Full day Phuket sightseen including , big budda , tiger park , view pont , chshunut factory ,Phuket old town ,souvenir shop ,chalong temple
  • Depending on your flight schedule, you can indulge in some last-minute shopping or sightseeing.
  • Private transfer to Phuket Airport for your departure.
